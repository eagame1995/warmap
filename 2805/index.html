Vocabulary Warm-up

Quick revision before reading / speaking. Match meanings, complete short contexts, then use the words in natural conversation.

Level: Pre-Intermediate
Adult learner
Time: 7–10 min
Focus: words from the text

Class timer

Use 3 minutes for Task 1, 3 minutes for Task 2, then discussion.

3. Speak for 30 seconds

Choose 2–3 questions. Try to use at least one target word in each answer.

Question 1

Have you ever had a part-time job? What was good or bad about it?

Question 2

What place near the sea or a river feels familiar to you?

Question 3

When was the last time you lost something important? Did it return?

Question 4

Which job sounds harder to you: a fisherman or a bricklayer? Why?

Question 5

Do you usually feel sorry for people quickly, or do you need to know the full story?

Question 6

Imagine something suddenly disappeared from your room. What would you check first?
