🎲 Speaking Production: The Weird Day Show

Students create and present a strange but possible day. The class listens and decides: Is this a good day, a crazy day, or a boring day?

Click the button to get your speaking mission.

Speaking frame

My day is strange. First, I .... Then I .... After that, I .... In my free time, I .... I think this day is great / crazy / boring because ....

Class vote

After each presentation, classmates vote with fingers:

1 finger = boring day   |   3 fingers = normal day   |   5 fingers = crazy but great day

Vocabulary: get up, have a shower, have breakfast/lunch/dinner, go to school, go home, do homework, go to bed, chat online, do sport, draw, go out with friends, listen to music, paint, play computer games, play the guitar/piano, read, surf the Internet, watch films/TV.
