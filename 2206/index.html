Present Simple

habits / facts / routines
  • I usually watch videos after school.
  • She plays volleyball on Fridays.
  • Use with: always, usually, often, sometimes, every day, on Mondays.

Present Continuous

now / around now / temporary
  • I am watching a video right now.
  • She is learning Spanish this month.
  • Use with: now, right now, today, at the moment, this week.
