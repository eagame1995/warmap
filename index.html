2. Choose & Speak

Choose 3–4 questions and write short answers, or discuss with a partner.

1. Are you a competitive person? In what situations?
2. Do you like to spend time apart from your friends or always together? Why?
3. Are you more independent or do you like help?
4. Do you sometimes feel jealous? When?
5. Are you shy when you meet new people?
6. Do you know any identical twins? Are they really the same?
